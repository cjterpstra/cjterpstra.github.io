Hobbies

Soccer

I have mentioned many times in this article that I love to play soccer. I play on Chicago Fire and I have been playing with some of my teammates since I was 7. Soccer is one of the things in life where it's unpredictable and anything can happen. That's why I love it. There is so much to look forward for and you have no idea how anything is going to play out. One minute you could be losing 2-1 and then the next minute you could be winning 3-2. That's what makes the game interesting for me.

Fortnite

Fortnite is a very fun game that my friends and I play together. I started playing on my friend's accounts and I now have a setup of my own. For me, fortnite is a way to get away from everbody and have a little fun with my friends. Fortnite is not just a game that you have to be good at, you can also play other maps to your liking. Fortnite is also a game that you can easily progress at with a little time. I've gotten much better over the years and it makes the game a lot more fun when you aren't always being eliminated.
